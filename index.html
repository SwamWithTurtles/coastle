How to Play

An unknown coaster is randomly chosen from a list of popular, well-known coasters. The list is very biased because I hand-picked them.

Type a coaster name into the search box and click the coaster/park you want to guess. Your guess can be any coaster and does not need to be in the popular coasters list.

There's four different tiles that can show up when you guess:

The answer is higher than your current guess.

The answer is lower than your current guess.

The answer doesn't match your current guess.

The answer matches your current guess.

*

Game ends when you guess the correct coaster or you use all six turns.
